## 🌟 The Kinly Paradigm: Key Capabilities

### 🛡️ 1. Absolute Client-Side Zero-Knowledge Privacy
Unlike mainstream ancestry websites that commercialize your DNA or monetize personal family profiles, Kinly puts you in complete control. 
*   **On-Device Encryption:** All sensitive fields—including names, birthdates, journal archives, and relation links—are encrypted directly in your browser using high-grade symmetric cryptography before they ever sync to the cloud.
*   **Unreadable to Third Parties:** The database never sees your family’s real identities. All stored records remain mathematical ciphertext, fully secured by keys held exclusively by you.

### 🌐 2. Dynamic, Interactive Family Trees
Reimagine your heritage with a beautiful, responsive canvas that adapts to your exploration:
*   **Drag-and-Drop Reparenting:** Restructure family relationships intuitively. Simply drag a family member’s node and drop it over another to instantly update parents and children with built-in cyclical loop prevention.
*   **Focus, Zoom, and Breadcrumbs:** Smoothly navigate dense lineages with canvas zooming and real-time generation breadcrumbs. Click on any member to trace their direct relationship path back to the root of your family line.
*   **Collapse and Expand:** Keep your visual space clean by collapsing or expanding entire branches of your ancestral tree with a single click.

### 🔍 3. Secure LinMatch (Anonymized Overlap Detection)
Discover missing branches and lost connections without exposing your private tree to strangers:
*   **Zero-Knowledge Hash Matching:** The LinMatch engine utilizes blind ancestral hashes and birth-year indices to detect potential overlaps with other trees on the network.
*   **Mutual Consent Inquiry:** If a matching segment is detected, you are notified of a potential lineage match while keeping all non-overlapping records securely locked and anonymous.

### 🏜️ 4. Interactive Cryptography Sandbox
Witness security in motion! The landing interface features an active sandbox showcasing how raw data is protected:
*   **Real-Time Ciphertext Translation:** Interact with floating constellation nodes to see raw, encrypted data stream from the server and watch it decrypt line-by-line right before your eyes in a safe-visual sandbox.

### 📸 5. Personalized Heirloom Media Vault
Create a stunning visual record of your ancestors:
*   **Traditional Corner-Ornamented Mounts:** Showcase historical photos with double-bordered heirloom mounts that echo physical, vintage photo albums.
*   **In-App Camera & Cropping:** Snap a portrait directly from your webcam or upload historic scans, then use the live rotation and crop controls to frame each family member beautifully.

### 📊 6. Bespoke Heritage Reports
Prepare professional documents for family reunions, archives, or printing:
*   **Granular Privacy Toggles:** Instantly generate clean, beautifully formatted PDF or print-ready reports. 
*   **Tailored Content:** Toggle specific contact information, private biographical diaries, and lateral relations on or off depending on who you are sharing the report with.

### ⏰ 7. Anniversary Alerts & Reminders
Keep your ancestors’ memory alive across generations:
*   **Dynamic Reminders:** Automatically track upcoming birthdays, weddings, and key historical anniversaries of past and present family members.
*   **Interactive Calendars:** View scheduled events directly within the dashboard to coordinate family celebrations and memorial moments.

### ✈️ 8. Zero-Friction Offline Sync
Never lose your progress when traveling to remote ancestral sites, local archives, or cemeteries:
*   **Local-First Design:** Write journal logs, upload documents, and adjust tree relations entirely offline.
*   **Automatic Cloud Replication:** As soon as you are reconnected to the internet, Kinly detects the active network and safely synchronizes your encrypted local database state back to the cloud.
